  • Okutama Car meet

    I came across this car meet in Okutama(奥多摩) which is the western most part of Tokyo prefecture. The mountain side town looks amazing during the autumn. But the best part were the classic cars and bikes constantly wandering around the mountains and I managed to get some pictures where some of those were stopped at a parking.


  • Mirai park Car meet

    I came across this car meet at the Mirai park in Saitama prefecture. I was on another road trip with my family when I came across this. There were many classic cars and most owners were happy to talk about and share their knowledge.
